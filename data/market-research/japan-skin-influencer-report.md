### Key 2025-2026 Trends

- **肌活 (Hadakatsu)**: Skin health optimization before makeup
- **Ingredient Analysis**: Certified influencers gaining trust
- **K-Beauty Influence**: Glass skin, layered skincare expanding in Japan
- **Male Skincare Mainstream**: No longer niche
- **Micro-Influencers**: Engagement > follower count
- **Brand Ownership**: Top influencers launching own product lines
- **Mute Makeup**: Natural, subdued makeup emphasizing skin quality

### Platform Dynamics

- **YouTube**: Educational/long-form. Doctor influencers dominate
- **TikTok**: Viral product discovery. Gen Z core channel
- **Instagram**: Before/after visual. Branding
- **Twitter/X**: Quick info sharing, ingredient discussion
- **Domestic (LIPS/@cosme)**: Trust validation, review-centric

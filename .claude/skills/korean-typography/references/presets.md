## Pairing Rules

- Keep body text readable first.
- Use display and handwriting fonts for headings, labels, stickers, or short accent phrases only.
- Add `NanumGothicCoding` for code, terminal, CLI, developer, or AI-agent surfaces.
- For long-form Korean reading, start from `MaruBuri`, `Noto Serif KR`, `Gowun Batang`, or `Noto Sans KR`.
- For repeated product UI, start from `Pretendard Variable`, `IBM Plex Sans KR`, `Spoqa Han Sans Neo`, `Goorm Sans`, or `Noto Sans KR`.
- For Korean dashboards, prefer one family plus weight/size contrast over decorative heading fonts.

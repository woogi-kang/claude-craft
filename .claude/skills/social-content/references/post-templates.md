# Post Templates & Hook Formulas

Ready-to-use templates for each platform. Fill in the brackets with your content.

## LinkedIn Templates

### Story Post (Highest Engagement)

```
[Hook: surprising outcome or lesson]

[2-3 sentences of context/setup]

[The turning point or key moment]

Here's what I learned:

1. [Lesson 1]
2. [Lesson 2]
3. [Lesson 3]

[Takeaway or question for the audience]

---
[Optional: CTA or link in comments note]
```

### Contrarian Take

```
[Bold contrarian statement]

Most people think [common belief].

But here's the thing:

[Your counter-argument with evidence]

[Supporting point 1]
[Supporting point 2]
[Supporting point 3]

The real lesson?

[Reframe the topic]

[Question to spark debate]
```

### Listicle / Tips

```
[Number] [things] that [desirable outcome]:

1. [Tip + brief explanation]

2. [Tip + brief explanation]

3. [Tip + brief explanation]

4. [Tip + brief explanation]

5. [Tip + brief explanation]

Which one resonates most? [Or: What would you add?]
```

### How-To / Educational

```
How to [achieve outcome] (step by step):

Step 1: [Action]
→ [Why it matters]

Step 2: [Action]
→ [Why it matters]

Step 3: [Action]
→ [Why it matters]

Step 4: [Action]
→ [Why it matters]

The key most people miss:

[Unique insight]

Save this for later. ♻️ Repost if it helps someone.
```

## Twitter/X Thread Templates

### Tutorial Thread

```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
How to [achieve desirable outcome] in [timeframe]:

(A thread 🧵)

Tweet 2:
Step 1: [First action]

[Explanation in 1-2 sentences]

[Visual or example if possible]

Tweet 3-8:
[Continue steps with same format]

Tweet 9 (Recap):
TL;DR:

1. [Step 1 summary]
2. [Step 2 summary]
3. [Step 3 summary]
...

Tweet 10 (CTA):
If you found this useful:

1. Follow me @[handle] for more [topic]
2. RT the first tweet to help others
3. [Optional: link to resource]
```

### Story Thread

```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
[Dramatic/surprising opening]

Here's how it happened: 🧵

Tweet 2:
[Set the scene - where/when/context]

Tweet 3-7:
[Build the narrative with tension]
[Each tweet should end with something that makes you want to read the next]

Tweet 8:
[The turning point / climax]

Tweet 9:
[Resolution and outcome]

Tweet 10:
[The lesson learned]

What would you have done differently?
```

### Analysis Thread

```
Tweet 1 (Hook):

Here's what the data shows: 🧵

Tweet 2:

[Data point or evidence]

Tweet 3-7:
[Continue findings with same format]

Tweet 8:
The biggest takeaway:

[Synthesis of all findings]

Tweet 9:
What this means for you:

[Practical application]

Tweet 10:
Want more analysis like this?

Follow @[handle] — I break down [topic] every [frequency].
```

## Instagram Templates

### Carousel (Educational)

```
Slide 1 (Cover):
[Bold headline that promises value]
[Subtitle: "Swipe to learn →"]

Slide 2:
[Problem statement or context]
"Most people think [common belief]..."

Slide 3-8:
[One key point per slide]
[Keep text minimal - under 30 words per slide]
[Use visuals/icons to support text]

Slide 9:
[Summary or recap of all points]

Slide 10 (CTA):
[Follow for more / Save this / Share with someone who needs this]
[@handle]
```

### Reel Script (30-60 seconds)

```
[0-3 sec] Hook:
"Stop doing [common mistake]" or "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]"

[3-10 sec] Setup:
"Most people [common approach]. But here's the problem..."

[10-40 sec] Value:
"Instead, try this:"
[Point 1]
[Point 2]
[Point 3]

[40-50 sec] Proof/Example:
"When I did this, [result]"

[50-60 sec] CTA:
"Follow for more [topic] tips"
"Save this for later"
```

## Hook Formulas (Extended)

### Curiosity Hooks
- "I was wrong about [common belief]."
- "The real reason [outcome] happens isn't what you think."
- "[Impressive result] — and it only took [surprisingly short time]."
- "Nobody talks about the dark side of [popular thing]."
- "What [successful person] does differently than everyone else:"

### Story Hooks
- "Last week, [unexpected thing] happened."
- "I almost [big mistake/failure]."
- "3 years ago, I [past state]. Today, [current state]."
- "The worst advice I ever received:"
- "I lost [something valuable] because of [mistake]."

### Value Hooks
- "How to [desirable outcome] (without [common pain]):"
- "[Number] [things] that [outcome]:"
- "Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead:"
- "The simplest way to [outcome] in [year]:"
- "A framework for [solving common problem]:"

### Contrarian Hooks
- "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]"
- "[Common advice] is wrong. Here's why:"
- "I stopped [common practice] and [positive result]."
- "Hot take: [industry trend] is overrated."
- "Why I disagree with [popular opinion]:"

### Social Proof Hooks
- "[Authority figure] taught me [lesson]. Here's how:"
- "After [working with/studying] [impressive number] of [things]:"
- "[Specific result] in [timeframe]. Here's the playbook:"
- "My most successful [clients/posts/campaigns] all do this:"

### Question Hooks
- "Why do most [professionals] fail at [thing]?"
- "What's the #1 mistake in [industry]?"
- "Is [common practice] actually hurting your [metric]?"

## CTA Formulas

### Engagement CTAs
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Agree or disagree? Drop a comment."
- "Tag someone who needs to see this."
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"

### Save/Share CTAs
- "Save this for when you need it."
- "Repost to help someone in your network."
- "Bookmark this thread."

### Follow CTAs
- "Follow for daily [topic] insights."
- "I share [topic] tips every [day]. Follow along."
- "Want more? Follow @[handle]."

### Conversion CTAs
- "Link in comments for the full [resource]."
- "DM me '[keyword]' for the template."
- "Free [resource] in my bio."
